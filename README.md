🎙️ Young Pen | Sound Archive

Silence was imposed, we started recording. Words weren't enough, sound took over. The writings ended, but we didn't stay silent.

🧠 What is this repository?

Young Pen may have been a magazine born within Yıldırım Beyazıt Project Anatolian High School.

But its characteristics… it's not just an archive.

Here:

The sound of suppressed particles, the echo of what overflows on the page, what begins where writing fails.

Reading is safe. Listening is confrontation.

🌍 About the Magazine

Young Pen doesn't belong to a single field. Because thought doesn't fit in one place.

In this magazine:

🔬 Science questions 🎨 Art evokes feelings 🧩 It deciphers the room 🧠 Psychology delves deep 📚 Philosophy disturbs

And we… we don't just write all this.

We do it.

🎧 Sound Archive

This repository is a rebirth of the content published in the magazine.

Their hallmarks:

The texts exist not to be read, but to be hissed; they don't just narrate, they bring to life, imbuing their words with tone, their imaginative breaths.

Because:

It begins with words on paper, and ends at the end.
